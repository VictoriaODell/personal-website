5Gyres

5Gyres is a non-profit with aims to help stop the pollution in the 5 Oceanic Gyres. They focus on outreach, education, and activism with particular interest in stopping plastic pollution.

Problem
5Gyres previous site was difficult to navigate and their brand lacked a feeling of action and energy.

Solution
In order to promote interest in their cause they needed a new identity that was powerful and stood out from similar non-profits and more user-friendly and navigatable site.
Typography & Color
Oceanic colors are both calm and refreshing. A punch of orange is inlcuded for call to actions. Bold and clean type provides clarity, reassurance, and gives a confident and urgent voice to the brand.
Making an Icon
It was important that 5Gyres be bold and memorable. The mark I created refrences the cyclical nature of water, recycling, and doing good. It also refrences the vortex like shape of the oceans gyres.
First Impressions
New one page based layout with major call to actions to help provide clarity and guide future donors and volunteers to the section that most interest and benefits them.
