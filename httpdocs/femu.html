FemU
Overview
FemU is a feminist organization at Texas State. They work to provide engaging dialouge, opportunities of activisim/volunteerism, and community outreach for feminist issues

Problem
FemU was lacking an identity to rally and operate under. With a lack of visibility on campus and online the organization was missing opportunites for growth and movement building.

Solution
Create a bold new identity and collateral for both print and online campaigning.
Setting the Mood
The identity for FemU has influence from the DIY and Riot girl feminism while implementing black and white imagery to give nods to the historical upbringing of the movement.
Typography & Color
They type for FemU is influence by collegiate atheletics to further emphasize power and longevity. Pink is prominent in an attempt to reclaim it as a color of power. Blue signifies trust and sociality.
A Mark that Matters
Going with a wordmark for FemU seemed to be the right chioce. Leaving out symbols allows for more room for inclusion. Bold, the FemU logo is loud and exudes Unapologetic confidence.
Statment Pieces
11x17 posters meant to arouse interest in the organization.Posters feature major feminist and forward thinkers with info about the FemU meetings.
Living Social
Social media graphics to help FemU promote it's brand, spread it's message, and find like minded individuls. It's important that FemU be online since a majority of it's market is active on Twitter, Tumblr, Instagram, & Facebook.
