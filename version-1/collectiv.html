Collectiv
Overview
Collectiv is an app that helps passionate people easily discover cool new products backed by their peers.

Problem
Users need a new way to make what they do more exciting. Finding new items that make work and hobbies more fun is usually done on Pinterest, Instagram, and through blogs and Google searches. However, those solutions usually require a fair amount of time because of the nature of their content.

Solution
Adding unique products to wishlist and following people who they admire and have similar intersts to is what Collectiv offers. Users can easily keep up with the trends and trinkets in their hobbies or professions.
Defining the User
Collectiv can house a diverse set of users but they all have one thing in common, they are driven to keep exploring and finding new products to make their life easier, more fun, and in some cases productive.
Typography & Color
Collectiv is for people with passion so naturally red was a great fit. Energyzing and powerful, those that use Collectiv should feel confident and bold. Neutral greys balance out the overpowring red and Exo is a rounded san-serif that makes the harsh colors less intimidating.
Profile and Products
View the profile and Product pages through the Invsion App! Just click the iPhone to get there.
Keeping Up
Collectiv's home screen can toggle between "For You". "Following", and "Explore". This allows for more chances for discovery and makes sures that fresh content is always at their fingertips.
Adding an item
Scan a barcode or manually input item info. From there add a picture from the gallery, web, or take one yourself.
Search and Explore
The explore tab has a search bar and catagories based on hobbies and interest. Users can look up a brand name, product name, or product type and stumble upon cool new finds posted by other users.

Jumping into a seach/catagory allows users to scroll through a gallery or list view of things that match or are simliar to what they're looking for.
