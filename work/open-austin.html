Open Austin

Open Austin is a volunteer citizen brigade advocating for open government, open data, and civic application development since 2009.


Problem

Open Austin had an amazing tech and policy community but needed extra help to promote the group and foster the community of citizen volunteers and activist.

Solution

I worked with the group to update the logo, assist with event organization, and collaborate on curating and creating content for the webiste and social media.
